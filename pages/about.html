Since childhood, I've been deeply passionate about creativity, which was sparked by my father's career as a mechanical engineer. This early influence inspired me to pursue mechanical engineering in university, aspiring to follow in his footsteps.


However, as I immersed myself in the field, I gradually realized its intricate and time-consuming nature. Seeking a more hands-on and immediately gratifying creative outlet, I discovered Swift, a language for iOS application development. Learning Swift allowed me to explore the logical thinking and endless creative possibilities that programming offers.


Driven by my desire to deepen my programming skills from the ground up and to pursue my longstanding interest in English, I made the decision to study and gain practical experience in Canada. This educational journey provided me with the opportunity to delve deeper into programming and broaden my creative horizons.
